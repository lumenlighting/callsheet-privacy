Privacy Policy

Callsheet  ·  Lumen Lighting And Event Design Corp  ·  Last updated: April 1, 2026

Callsheet is a professional event management app built for audio-visual and event production companies. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

Callsheet does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All data you enter into the app — including project details, client names, crew members, gear lists, and event information — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

2. Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate any servers, databases, or cloud infrastructure. We have no access to any data you create within the app.

3. Data You Export

Callsheet allows you to export PDF detail sheets and JSON backup files. These exports are initiated entirely by you and shared through Apple's standard share sheet. We have no involvement in or visibility into where you choose to send these files.

4. Third-Party Services

Callsheet does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not use crash reporting tools, usage analytics, or any SDKs that collect user data.

5. Children's Privacy

Callsheet is designed for professional use by adults in the event production industry. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, the new version will be posted at this URL with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
